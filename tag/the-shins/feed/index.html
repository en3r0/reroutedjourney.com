I know. Summer was a really tough time for us as I was sick for all of it and when we did things it was a painful experience so I didn’t feel like writing or blogging about them. The pain is but a memory and as I recover I’m being brave and looking back on the days that were the darkest. Jesus somehow remains faithful and offers glimpses of hope even in the valley of death. Among being in the hospital a ton and not being able to get out of bed most days we did do some fun activities this summer. Dustin bought my concert tickets to The Shins, my favorite band and we went to the Asian Festival in Columbus with some of our friends, Marlaina and Richard. We also managed to get some HUGE ice cream cones at a country store. Enjoy!
